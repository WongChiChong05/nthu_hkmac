The operational mission of the HK-Macau Student Association at NTHU:

Fostering Internal Connections among HK-Macau Students

X

How...



By organizing well-planned activities, we aim to strengthen interactions among students from Hong Kong and Macau, building a closely-knit network within the university.

Supporting Freshmen from HK-Macau in Adapting to Campus Life

X

How...



We provide comprehensive assistance, from living arrangements to academic support, ensuring that new students can seamlessly integrate into NTHU's academic and social environment.

Promoting HK-Macau Culture on Campus

X

How...



Through cultural events and themed seminars, we systematically introduce and showcase the unique cultural aspects of Hong Kong and Macau, fostering cross-cultural exchange and understanding.

Safeguarding the Rights and Interests of HK-Macau Students

X

How...



Representing the voice of HK-Macau students, we actively communicate with the university to secure fair resource allocation and policy support.

Enhancing Academic and Career Development for HK-Macau Students

X

How...



We provide platforms for academic exchange and career development resources, helping HK-Macau students to enhance their academic capabilities and achieve success in their professional endeavors. These objectives are dedicated to creating an efficiently run organization that offers comprehensive support to HK-Macau students, ensuring they achieve their fullest potential and thrive within the NTHU campus.
